Rules & Basics

Positions

Offense has a quarterback, running backs, wide receivers, tight ends, and linemen. Defense has linemen, linebackers, and defensive backs. Special teams handle kicking and returns.

Scoring

  • Touchdown: 6 points
  • Extra point (kick): 1 point
  • Two-point conversion: 2 points
  • Field goal: 3 points
  • Safety: 2 points

Common Penalties

False start (offense moves early), holding (illegal restraining), and pass interference (contact before the ball arrives) are among the most frequent calls.
